What to notice

The same visible signal does not automatically carry the same meaning. Your role determines how you interpret it, and that interpretation can change as time passes.

What this shows: A simplified demonstration that context affects meaning.
What this doesn't show: Real traffic laws, all intersection types, or every possible traffic signal.
